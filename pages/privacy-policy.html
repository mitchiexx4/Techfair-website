Privacy Policy

This sample privacy policy explains how the GIMPA SOTSS Tech Fair website may collect, use, store, and protect visitor information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly through registration forms, contact requests, event participation forms, download requests, and admin interactions. This may include your name, email address, phone number, organization, project details, and other information you choose to submit.

We may also collect basic technical information automatically when you use the website, such as browser type, device type, IP address, and general usage data that helps us understand site performance and improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through the website may be used to manage event registration, communicate important event updates, respond to enquiries, review project submissions, support downloads, improve website functionality, and maintain website security.

Where appropriate, we may also use contact details to send essential event reminders, schedule updates, exhibitor notices, or administrative messages connected to the Tech Fair.

3. Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with authorized event organizers, administrators, service providers, or institutional teams who need access to support the Tech Fair, maintain the website, or deliver related services.

We retain information only for as long as it is reasonably needed for event planning, administration, reporting, support, or legal and operational requirements.

4. Your Choices

You may contact the Tech Fair team to request updates to your submitted information, ask questions about how your information is used, or request removal where appropriate and legally permitted.
