Sponsorship Benefits

Aligned sponsorship details from the Tech Fair presentation for partners and supporters.

Why This Matters?

  • Practical, industry-relevant student exposure.
  • Opportunity for students to receive feedback, funding and professional mentorship.
  • Collaboration between GIMPA and industry.
  • Supports student projects to scale beyond the classroom.
  • Enhances employability, entrepreneurship, and research interest.

Programme Components

  • Opening Ceremony: Welcome and keynote address.
  • Project Presentations: Live demos, poster sessions, and Q&A.
  • Judging & Evaluation: Industry and faculty panel.
  • Panel Discussions / Mini Workshops: Digital transformation, entrepreneurship, and emerging technologies.
  • Awards & Networking Session
