Packages

Select the package that best matches your exhibitor or sponsor goals.

Diamond Pack

  • Headline logo placement across all event media and stage visuals.
  • Premium exhibition space in a top-traffic zone.
  • Keynote or featured speaking slot (subject to programme confirmation).
  • Dedicated recruitment and engagement booth support.
  • Priority judging panel invitation and award presentation opportunity.

Gold Pack

  • High-visibility logo placement on event banners and digital promotions.
  • Large exhibitor booth with power and branding support.
  • Mention in opening and closing ceremony acknowledgements.
  • Access to curated networking sessions with student innovators.
  • Priority access to sponsorship and media opportunities.

Silver Pack

  • Standard logo placement on selected event materials.
  • Standard exhibition booth allocation.
  • Brand mention in sponsor recognition announcements.
  • Access to exhibitor networking and product showcase sessions.
  • Post-event appreciation listing in programme summary.

Bronze Pack

  • Entry-level logo placement in partner listing channels.
  • Shared or compact exhibitor visibility option.
  • Inclusion in official sponsor roll call.
  • Access to general networking opportunities.
