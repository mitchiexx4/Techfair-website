Code of Conduct

This sample code of conduct sets expectations for attendees, speakers, exhibitors, sponsors, staff, volunteers, and guests participating in the GIMPA SOTSS Tech Fair.

1. Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a professional, respectful, inclusive, and safe environment for everyone participating in the Tech Fair, whether in person, online, or through event-related communication channels.

2. Expected Behaviour

  • Treat all participants with courtesy, professionalism, and respect.
  • Encourage constructive discussion, learning, and collaboration.
  • Respect personal boundaries, venue rules, and organizer instructions.
  • Help maintain a welcoming environment for people of different backgrounds, experiences, and viewpoints.

3. Unacceptable Behaviour

  • Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or abusive language.
  • Disruptive conduct during presentations, workshops, judging, or networking activities.
  • Unwanted physical contact, stalking, threatening behaviour, or inappropriate recording of others.
  • Damage to venue property, equipment, exhibitor materials, or digital systems.

4. Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behaviour that threatens safety or violates this code, please report it to event organizers as soon as possible so they can review the matter and respond appropriately.

  • Email: techfair@gimpa.edu.gh
  • Phone: 0202798583

5. Enforcement

Organizers may take reasonable action in response to misconduct, including warnings, removal from specific activities, restrictions on participation, or removal from the event where necessary.

This page is a sample code of conduct and should be reviewed before it is adopted as the final event policy.
