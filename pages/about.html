About the GIMPA Tech Fair

The School of Technology and Social Sciences (SOTSS) proudly presents the GIMPA Tech Fair & Industry Showcase, a flagship initiative designed to bridge academia and industry through practical, technology-enabled problem-solving. Under the 2026 theme, "Innovating for Impact: Technology Solutions for Ghana's Future," the fair gives students a platform to present bold ideas, engage industry leaders, and unlock collaboration, mentorship, and investment opportunities.

An Innovative Experience

The fair brings together academia, industry, government, and the broader innovation ecosystem to spotlight digital solutions for real-world challenges in healthcare, finance, agriculture, governance, cybersecurity, sustainability, education, and smart systems.

Why Participate?

  • Showcase your innovation to industry leaders and potential investors.
  • Gain expert feedback, mentorship, and visibility for your work.
  • Compete for awards, sponsorships, and national recognition.
  • Build networks for internships, research, and career opportunities.
  • Contribute to Ghana's digital transformation through practical solutions.

Programme Highlights

  • Official Launch: April 16, 2026.
  • Tech Festival: Hackathon, e-games, and tech talks on April 16-17, 2026.
  • Project Review: Proposals shortlisted by May 10, 2026.
  • Main Exhibition: Live demos, poster presentations, and interactive judging on June 3, 2026.
  • Venue: GIMPA, Greenhill Campus.

Who Can Apply?

  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students from accredited Ghanaian universities.
  • Individual and team-based submissions are both welcome.
  • Projects must be IT-based or technology-enabled solutions.
