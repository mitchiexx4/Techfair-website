About the GIMPA Tech Fair

The School of Technology and Social Sciences (SOTSS) proudly presents the GIMPA Tech Fair & Industry Showcase, a flagship annual event designed to bridge the gap between academia and industry. This premier platform provides students with the opportunity to exhibit innovative, industry-driven IT projects that demonstrate cutting-edge solutions to real-world challenges. The Tech Fair champions innovation, creativity, and problem-solving, while showcasing the remarkable talent emerging from Ghana's technology ecosystem. By bringing together students, industry professionals, innovators, and thought leaders, the event plays a vital role in cultivating digital talent and contributing to national development through technology and innovation.
