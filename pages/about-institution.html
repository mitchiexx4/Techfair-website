About GIMPA School of Technology and Social Sciences

The Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (GIMPA) School of Technology and Social Sciences is committed to providing world-class education and research in technology and social sciences, fostering innovation, academic excellence, and leadership for national and global development. Our vision is to position the School as a center of excellence in research, teaching, and professional training while driving innovation, increasing revenue, and enhancing operational efficiency. With a dedicated faculty and staff, diverse student body, and comprehensive programmes across various focus areas, we are at the forefront of preparing the next generation of technology leaders and innovators. The GIMPA SOTSS Tech Fair exemplifies our commitment to showcasing cutting-edge innovation, bridging the gap between academia and industry, and providing a platform for students, professionals, and industry leaders to collaborate and shape the future of technology in Ghana and beyond.
