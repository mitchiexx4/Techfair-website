Exhibitor Overview

Everything project exhibitors need to prepare for the GIMPA Tech Fair & Industry Showcase 2026 at Greenhill Campus.

Who Can Exhibit?

  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students from accredited Ghanaian universities.
  • Individual innovators and team-based project groups.
  • Projects that are IT-based or technology-enabled solutions.

Recommended Focus Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Analytics
  • Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics
  • Financial Technology & Digital Economy
  • Health Technology & Digital Health
  • Agricultural Technology, Sustainability, E-Governance, IoT, and EdTech

Exhibitor Benefits

  • Showcase your solution to industry leaders and potential investors.
  • Receive expert feedback, mentorship, and public recognition.
  • Build networks for internships, research, and career opportunities.
  • Position your work within Ghana's broader digital transformation agenda.

Selection & Exhibition Format

  • All submissions go through review and shortlisting.
  • Only selected projects are invited to the main exhibition on June 3, 2026.
  • Selected teams present through live demos, poster displays, and judge interaction.
